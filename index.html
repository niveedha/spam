ABOUT THE REGIONAL CAMPUS, TIRUNELVELI


The Regional Campus Tirunelveli of Anna University was established during 2007. It offers higher education in Engineering, Technology and management. Located in an Arcadian environment and away from the hustle and bustle crowd of the city. The Regional Campus Tirunelveli is equipped with excellent infrastructure and highly qualified members of faculty, who facilitate hands on experience to students to master their skills in various disciplines.
